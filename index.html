About Lauren

Strategy from someone who’s built it before.

StoryWork is a strategy practice founded by Lauren Shippy. Over the last decade-plus, our work has lived at the intersection of operations, capital, and brand — helping build a private equity real estate firm from the ground up, co-leading a family investment office, and serving in COO and operating roles for multiple startups across sectors.

That mix is unusual on purpose. It’s why the strategy we bring isn’t academic — it’s shaped by what it actually takes to launch, fund, scale, and steward a business through real decisions.

Lauren is the author of Seen, Known + Heard, host of the Behind the Curtain podcast, and writes regularly on Substack. She served on the leadership team of the Palm Beach Women’s Network for over five years and lives in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, with her husband and two daughters.

Through StoryWork, we partner with companies and founders at the moments that decide what comes next.
